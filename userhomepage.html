How does it work?

FindThere is designed to streamline the process of recovering lost items within our campus community. Users can easily create posts to report lost or found items through the dedicated platform. When a user discovers a lost item, they can submit a detailed post, providing essential information about the item and its location. For those who have found items, they can send proof to the system administrator, usually located in the security office, to facilitate the return process. Additionally, owners of lost items can initiate a request to claim their belongings from the admin at the security office. This system not only enhances the efficiency of reuniting individuals with their lost possessions but also ensures a secure and organized approach to handling lost and found items within our university community.
